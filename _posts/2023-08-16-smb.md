Using Nmap to scan Netbios ports (quicker to isolate ips first with a sweep)

    $ nmap -v -p 139,445 -oG smb.txt 192.168.50.1-254

Nbtscan to isolate netbios ports 

    $ sudo nbtscan -r 192.168.50.0/24
